Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What is the difference between Hand Polish and PU Finish?
Hand polish is a traditional method that gives a natural, warm look to wood and is easier to repair. PU (Polyurethane) finish creates a thicker, more durable protective layer that is water-resistant and available in various sheen levels from matte to high gloss.
Do you offer onsite polishing services?
Yes, absolutely! We have a dedicated team of expert craftsmen who can come to your home or office to perform the polishing work. We take great care to protect your surroundings while working onsite.
How long does a typical furniture restoration take?
The timeline varies depending on the condition and size of the furniture. A standard dining table restoration might take 3-5 days. We provide a specific estimated completion date after inspecting your items.
Can you match the color of my existing furniture?
Yes, our "Custom Finishes" service is designed exactly for this. We can create custom stains and color blends to match your new pieces with your existing furniture set perfectly.
